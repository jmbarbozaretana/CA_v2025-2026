La expresión para la frecuencia de resonancia $f_0$ es:

\aBcde
{$f_0=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\dfrac{R^2}{L^2}-\dfrac{1}{LC}}$\medskip}
{$f_0=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{LC}-\dfrac{R^2}{L^2}}$\medskip}
{$f_0=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{LC}-\dfrac{R^2}{L^2C}}$\medskip}
{$f_0=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{L^2C}-\dfrac{R^2}{L^2}}$\medskip}
{$f_0=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\dfrac{R^2}{L^2}-\dfrac{1}{L}}$\medskip}
